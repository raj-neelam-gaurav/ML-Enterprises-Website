About ML Enterprises

Since 1972, ML Enterprises LLP has been at the forefront of India's infrastructure development. From our humble beginnings as M/s. Sis Ram Bir Singh in Rewari, Haryana, we've evolved into a comprehensive EPC powerhouse.

We specialize in Railway & Siding Works, PEB Warehouse Projects, Electrical Works, FFS Works, Silo Projects, and comprehensive Civil Construction across India.

Our commitment: Excellence in execution, timely delivery, and building relationships based on mutual trust.
